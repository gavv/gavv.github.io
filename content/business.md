# Roc Streaming

I'm a co-founder and core maintainer of [Roc Streaming](https://roc-streaming.org/).

It is a growing, completely open-source ecosystem for real-time audio streaming, including libraries, tools, streaming daemons, sound server modules / virtual devices, and end-user apps.

I provide personal contract-based services including:

- Turnkey or custom solutions built on Roc Streaming ecosystem
- Development of specific features
- Help with integration into customer products
- Prepaid support hours
- Technical consulting

Please reach me at victor@enise.org.

# ViveSound

I'm a co-founder of [ViveSound](https://vivesound.com/).

The company builds a commercial audio engine and SDK that allows full control over system-wide audio and injecting custom processing at the OS level.

The company provides the following technologies and services:

- Licensing of the audio engine and SDK to customers
- White-label solutions based on the customer's brand and processing algorithm
- Various end-user apps

Please contact us at support@vivesound.com.
